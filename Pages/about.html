I'm Ari, and I've been making movies ever since I can remember. I got my hands on my dad's camera in fifth grade, and from there, filmmaking became my passion. At just 10 years old, I started creating my own short films, learning as I went along. I've dedicated my free time to mastering cinematography, editing, and storytelling. I've also had the chance to work on professional film sets, learning from industry pros and bringing that experience to my own projects. I've written, directed, and produced my own movies and advertisements. I'm just getting started on this filmmaking journey, and every project is a chance to push my limits and tell meaningful stories. Join me in exploring the world of storytelling through film.
